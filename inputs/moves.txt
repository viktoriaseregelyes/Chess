all piece rule:
-become king when hit
-move forward when hit

pawn rule:
general move: forward, backward, right, left
-move again when hit
-move anywhere 2 times in the game

rook rule:
general move: forward, backward, right, left
-move forward when hit
-move anywhere 3 times in the game

bishop rule:
general move: 1 forward 1 left, 1 forward 1 right, left
-become queen when hit

king rule:
general move: 2 forward left, forward, backward, right, left
-move anywhere 2 times in the game
